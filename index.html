About us

Clarity and support, for every student.

Magizhvagam is a non-profit organisation working to make quality career guidance and personal development support accessible to students in government and under-resourced schools — many of whom otherwise never get access to structured counselling. We believe every child deserves clarity about their strengths, their options, and their future, regardless of their school's resources.

"Every individual and community, valued, supported, and given the opportunity to thrive — with dignity, well-being, and hope."

Our Mission

To drive measurable and meaningful social change through impactful initiatives centred on education, environmental sustainability, and community development. By combining compassionate action with evidence-based, community-led approaches, we strive to create long-term impact — visible in transformed lives, empowered communities, and measurable, sustainable outcomes. Alongside our core focus areas, we undertake diverse social welfare initiatives that address evolving community needs.

Our Vision

To build a compassionate and inclusive society where every individual and community is valued, supported, and given the opportunity to thrive with dignity, well-being, and hope — free from neglect and inequality.

Programs & Initiatives

Where our work is happening.

Each initiative starts with a real, specific need — a room, a session, an afternoon — and builds outward from there.

Initiative 01

Project Aadhyam

Setting up a vibrant library and upgrading infrastructure at Thanthai Periyar Government School, Puzhuthivakkam — home to more than 520 bright students. We're creating a comfortable, inspiring space that fosters a lifelong love of reading, with sturdy library racks, four ceiling fans, four LED tube lights, and window installations for the computer lab. We're also expanding the library room with more storage racks and a growing book collection.

520+ students Est. budget ₹74,500 Puzhuthivakkam
Initiative 02

Project Puvi

Conducted at Thanthai Periyar Government School, Project Puvi brought 11th and 12th grade students together to learn about current environmental issues and sustainable living, encouraging them to become responsible environmental stewards. A "Save Earth" drawing competition engaged middle schoolers with creativity and thoughtful ideas on protecting our planet, with certificates for all participants and medals for the winners. The programme closed with a tree-planting initiative — a shared commitment to preserving the environment for generations to come.

Environmental awareness Drawing competition Tree planting
Initiative 03

Yoga & Meditation for Akshaya Trust

On International Yoga Day, the Magizhvagam team had the privilege of conducting a meditation and yoga workshop for the elderly residents of Akshaya Trust, Velappanchavadi. Our heartfelt thanks to Mr. Hemanth J for leading the yoga session with dedication and care, and to Mr. Ajayhritik for guiding residents through a calming, mindful meditation. At Magizhvagam, we believe wellness and inner peace are for all ages — it was a joy spending this special day promoting health, mindfulness, and happiness with the residents.
